### 1. *Symptoms:*
* Persistent cough for two weeks
* Occasional headaches
* Slight fever
* Shortness of breath when walking up stairs (breathlessness)
* Low appetite
* No significant weight loss, but slight decrease in appetite

### 2. *Prescriptions:*
* Antibiotic: Amoxicillin 500 milligrams twice daily for seven days
* Cough syrup: Dextramythorfin 10 milliliters twice daily
* Fever relief: Paracetamol 500 milligrams as needed (every six hours)
* Multivitamins once daily to boost immunity

### 3. *Lab Tests:*
* Complete Blood Count (CBC) 
* Chest X-ray
* COVID-19 test
* Thyroid function test

### 4. *General Health Advice:*
* Incorporate at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise into your daily routine
* Limit processed food and focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole foods
* Ensure adequate fluid intake
